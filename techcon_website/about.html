Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the largest technology conferences in East Africa, attracting innovators, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders from around the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of tech innovators through education, collaboration, and inspiration. We aim to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and real-world applications, ensuring that tech serves humanity’s greatest needs.

Notable Past Speakers

Jane Mwangi

CEO of Nairobi Tech Labs, Jane has been a pioneer in AI-driven healthcare solutions. Her keynote on “AI for Good” in 2022 inspired a wave of socially responsible innovation.

David Kimani

A cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet Africa, David’s talks on digital resilience have helped startups strengthen their security infrastructure.

Maria Ochieng

Renowned software engineer and open-source advocate. Her 2023 session on “Women in Tech” encouraged more inclusivity in the industry.
