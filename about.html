Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago with the mission of bringing together innovators, developers, and tech leaders from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into one of the premier technology conferences, inspiring countless professionals and students alike.

Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our goal is to foster collaboration, ignite innovation, and empower individuals to create technologies that shape the future. We are committed to building an inclusive and forward-thinking tech community.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace, a pioneer in computer science, inspired attendees with her vision of computational creativity and problem-solving.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing, known for his groundbreaking work in artificial intelligence, discussed the evolution of AI and its impact on society.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper, an innovator in programming languages, shared her journey in software development and encouraged future generations of coders.
